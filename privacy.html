Privacy Policy

Welcome to TobillionHomes. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our platform for property search, mortgage estimation, and real estate transactions in Kenya.

1. Information Collection

We collect information that you provide directly to us when creating an account, inquiring about a listing, or subscribing to our news. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and preferences regarding property types and locations within Kenyan counties.

2. Data Usage

We use the data to provide high-quality real estate recommendations and to facilitate communication between buyers, sellers, and property managers. Your data helps us improve the precision of our property valuation algorithms and market trend reports.

Key Data Protection Note:

TobillionHomes strictly complies with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019. We do not sell your personal data to third-party marketing firms.

3. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard encryption protocols (SSL/TLS) to ensure that your financial inquiries and personal identities are shielded from unauthorized access. Our database is hosted on high-security digital-first infrastructure designed for the Kenyan market.
