Property Description

Set within the exclusive Miotoni enclave of Karen, this architectural masterpiece represents the pinnacle of modern luxury living in Nairobi. Designed by award-winning architects, the villa balances brutalist concrete forms with the warmth of natural Kenyan timber and local stone.

The ground floor features a dramatic double-height foyer leading to an expansive open-plan living area. Floor-to-ceiling sliding glass doors dissolve the boundary between the interior and the manicured lawns. A chef's kitchen, equipped with top-tier German appliances and a discrete scullery, caters to high-end entertaining.

Upstairs, four palatial en-suite bedrooms offer sanctuary and privacy. The primary suite includes a private terrace overlooking the Ngong Hills, a walk-in dressing room, and a spa-like bathroom with a freestanding stone tub. This property is more than a home; it is a legacy investment in Kenya's most prestigious neighborhood.
